The Vitl Bottle

The perfect accessory to help remind you to stay hydrated and to take your vitamins!

This handy 500ml reusable bottle is made using double-walled vacuum insulation technology to keep your cold drinks cold and hot drinks hot - ideal for keeping you cool during a workout or warm on a winter’s day.

The powder coating prevents condensation from building up on its surface and its bold colour will help remind you to take your vitamins and stay hydrated.

Features

Temperature controlled

To preserve flavour and freshness, your Vitl bottle is made using TrueVacuum™ technology so liquids will be kept ice cold for up to 24 hours or piping hot for up to 12 hours.

Eco-friendly

Say goodbye to plastic bottles for good. Your reusable bottle is made using BPA-free 18/8 stainless steel, keeping it protected from rust and lingering flavours so you can fill it with anything from milk to coffee or soup!

Reliable

Designed to be taken with you on the go, the bottle’s airtight screw-top cap saves you from unexpected leaks soaking your bag, and the hard-wearing powder coating prevents condensation from building up on the outside.
